Theatre

When I was in middle school and high school, my family and I spent countless hours driving to and from rehearsals from shows. I enjoyed every show I was a part of, and I loved learning from all of the cast and crew members I worked with.

I’d like to give a huge shoutout and thank you to my family for always being supportive of my theatre pastime and for always being willing to help me out with driving, practicing, and coming to see my shows!
