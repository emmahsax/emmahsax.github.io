Dear Wedding Diary,

The final numbers are in, and we’re now able to see exactly how much $ was spent and in which categories. I’ll list them out by category, and add up a total for each section.

One thing that I like to note (if only to myself) is that some categories listed here aren’t included in a common wedding budget (e.g. engagement ring, prenup). But, my grand total will include them anyway for completion’s sake.

In addition, it’s valuable to note that this was a local wedding (not destination), and was located in the Twin Cities, Minnesota (since all of that affects costs).
